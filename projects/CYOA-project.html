CYOA Project

A Stereotypical Rich White Girl Life

Context

Thursday, February 08, 2024

For this project I was assigned to a partner, Bianca, where we both have to choose an adventure and make choices that leads you to a different outcome.

Content

Me and my partner, Bianca, decided to make something based of a movie. We went for the topic "A Stereotypical Rich White Girl Life" where you wake up as a white girl and go through a stereotypical life as a white girl such as going to sephora and claires.

Reflection

This project was easy at first because we were able to communicate with each other but then the semester ended which it became really difficult. We got new schedule and me and my partner Bianca no longer had the same SEP period which made it difficult to ask each other for help and give information on what to do. It also became really hard to know when to pull the others person work without having and conflicts with each others work being done.

My takeaways with this projects was being able to communicate through texting with someone who was a different period with me. Me and Bianca will send texts to each other on where we stopped and what's the next thing we should do like "git pull". At the end we both were able to finish this project with a good storyplot and outline.
